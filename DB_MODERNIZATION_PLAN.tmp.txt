Database Modernization Execution Plan (Temporary Tracker)
Owner: OpenCode
Date: 2026-04-23

Decision Defaults (locked)
- Repositories are internal composition details and primarily injected into services.
- Routes and middleware consume services only (no direct DB surface).
- Canonical low-level contract is DbFacade: raw(), withClient(), withTransaction().
- Raw pool stays only for infra integrations (session store, health/pool metrics, migrations/scripts).
- Compatibility layers are removed progressively by seam once migration is complete for that seam.

Global Constraints
- Every commit must keep production behavior intact.
- Every commit is CI-gated before continuing.
- No new legacy surfaces may be introduced.
